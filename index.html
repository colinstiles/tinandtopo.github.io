Hi there. I'm a geospatial analyst and web developer based in Chattanooga, TN, with a passion for building interactive and beautiful web maps.

About

My name is Tin, and I'm a passionate advocate for open-source mapping and data visualization. I believe that good maps should be accessible to everyone, and I'm dedicated to building tools that help people explore and understand the world around them. My work focuses on combining clean, modern design with powerful geospatial data to create engaging and informative experiences.

This website is a portfolio of my projects, experiments, and the various digital maps I've created. I'm always learning and building new things, so check back often for new work!
